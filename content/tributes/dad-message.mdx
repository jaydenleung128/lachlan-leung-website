Lachlan - Locky - LokLok - You left us far too early, but not without reasons.<br/>
You brought us happiness and drew our family closer than ever before.<br/>
You were simply the best kid, a son beyond anything I could have dreamed of.

You excelled at school and were a gifted champion in table tennis. I will deeply miss watching you play.<br/>
As an accredited table tennis coach, you shared your advice with me, and I will remember it for the rest of my life.

You faced your treatment with extraordinary courage and maturity.<br/>
You understood every step required to keep yourself comfortable during treatment. How to minimise pain, the purpose of each medication, and their side effects. Despite everything, you remained resilient through every challenge.

You cared deeply for Mum and Dad. You kept a notebook of all the questions we couldn't answer, so we wouldn't worry.<br/>
I remember when Locky told Mum to go home and rest, even though we knew Locky wanted mum to stay with him at the hospital.

We are deeply grateful to the neurosurgeons at St George Hospital and the Oncologist doctors and nurses at The Children's Hospital Randwick. Lachlan received truly world-class care.

I would like to thank everyone who donated to the GoFundMe. All proceeds will go to the Children's Cancer Institute, where we are able to nominate the type of cancer research supported.

Thank you also to everyone who has attended Lachlan's funeral service today. Your presence means more to us than words can express.

In closing, I'd like you to remember a phrase Lachlan often said to me… when things were difficult… or when we were under pressure.

He would look at me and say… "BaBa Relax", "BaBa Relax"<br/>
That was Lachlan… calming… reassuring… letting you know that everything was going to be okay.<br/>
Relax, Relax, Relax
